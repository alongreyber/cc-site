Our philosophy
Please be assured that all Crocodile Creek products are safe for children. We regard the safety
of the children in our world and the safety of our products as being of the utmost importance.

Our product development team works closely with our manufacturing facilities to see that our products conform to all of the appropriate safety standards. Our products are tested with Third Party Accredited testing laboratories. Certificates of Compliance for our current items can be found on this page of the website.

Backpacks
Our backpacks are made of high-quality polyester and are BPA-free, PVC-free and phthalate-free.

Drinking Bottles
Our drinking bottles are made of high-quality stainless steel #304 which does not require any
type of protective coating/lining. The lid is HDPE#2 and the cap is PP#5. All materials are completely recyclable and are lead-free, phthalate-free, BPA-free and PVC free.

Food Jars
Our food jars are made of high-quality stainless steel #304 which does not require any type of protective coating/lining. The cap and trim are polypropylene.

Ice Packs
The gel in our ice packs is 93% water with 4% sodium carboxymethyl cellulose.
The exterior sleeve of the ice pack is a blend of PE and PP (polyethylene and polypropylene).

Lunch Boxes
Our lunch boxes are child safe with lead-free linings and are PVC-free and phthalate-free.

Placemats
Our placemats are made of polypropylene and are PVC-free.

Playground Balls
Our playground balls are made of natural and synthetic rubber and contain no PVC, BPA or vinyl.

Sandwich & Snack Keepers
Our sandwich and snack keepers are made of polypropylene and are BPA-free.

If you have any additional questions about these or other Crocodile Creek products,
please don't hesitate to contact us.
