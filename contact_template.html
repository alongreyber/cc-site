Contact Us / FAQ's

At Crocodile Creek, we care about you and all of our customers and want to provide you with the best possible customer service. Below you will find the answers to some of the more frequently asked questions and a form to contact our team.


What do I do if I think there is a quality concern with my Crocodile Creek product?

Crocodile Creek stands behind all our products and we are happy to replace products that might have a manufacturing defect. Replacement request must be received within 30 days of purchasing.

If you have purchased an item at your local retailer or an online store, please contact the retailer or the specific online site for an exchange or return.

Please note that we cannot ship replacements outside of the U.S. and Canada.


I just put together my Crocodile Creek puzzle, and It’s missing a piece. Can you send me the missing piece?

If on the rare occurrence it is discovered that a puzzle piece is missing when first assembled, we will send a puzzle replacement. Please fill out the form below with the required information, and we’ll get you your replacement puzzle as quickly as possible.


How can I replace a product that I no longer see available on the Crocodile Creek website?

Unfortunately, we no longer manufacture certain products and cannot replace items that are no longer in stock. Crocodile Creek does not carry additional inventory for our Back-to-School and eating accessories. For these discontinued items, please contact the retailer or online site where you originally purchased the item.


Do you still have questions for us?
